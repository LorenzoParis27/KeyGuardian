How the checker works

Hackers have various methods to crack passwords, but our password strength and time-to-hack checker is based on brute-force attacks. In a brute-force attack, software generates an endless array of character combinations until it successfully guesses the password. Notably, our system can attempt more than 100 million combinations every second!

Dictionaries attack and other methods

Beyond ordinary bruteforce attack, hackers use other methods to guess your password. One of them is dictionary attack, where thay pass trought a list of common password or dictionary words. Even though changing "a" with 4,"e" with 3 and so on is a good idea, hackers know well this trick.
